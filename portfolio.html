AutoSim

A Racing Video Game

AutoSim is a game I have been developing since March of 2020. A fully immersive AI-driven racing management simulator, AutoSim takes players into the business of auto racing. Start a team or inherit a pre-exiting team, hire drivers, upgrade your cars, and compete for the championship. Or, go hands off and simulate a what-if scenario up to 60 years using historical rosters. The extensive mod-friendly approach to this game allows users to get the most out of their experience. The possibilities are limitless with AutoSim.
